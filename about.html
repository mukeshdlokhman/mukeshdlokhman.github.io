About DoctorsMitra

Empowering doctors with intelligent technology to deliver safer, faster, and smarter healthcare.

Our History

How DoctorsMitra began and evolved into an AI-powered healthcare platform

Our Beginning

DoctorsMitra was conceived as part of the DockerMitra Hospital Management System with a clear vision: to reduce clinical documentation burden and enhance decision-making for doctors.

Our Evolution

With growing adoption of digital healthcare and telemedicine, DoctorsMitra evolved into an AI-assisted doctor companion, integrating smart prescriptions, patient history tracking, and clinical safety checks.

Company Profile

Who we are and what we stand for

Who We Are

DoctorsMitra is a healthcare technology solution designed specifically for doctors, clinics, and hospitals to streamline consultations and improve patient outcomes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to assist doctors with intelligent tools that enhance efficiency, reduce errors, and allow them to focus more on patient care rather than paperwork.

Our Vision

To become a trusted AI-powered clinical assistant used by healthcare professionals across India and globally.
